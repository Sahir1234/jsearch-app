What Is This App?

This app is built around the jService API, which allows users to quickly query an archive of past Jeopardy questions. You can use the filter bar to look for specific types of questions based on their value, airdate or category. You can even get a random batch of 1 to 100 questions if you'd like. Please note that since the API sometimes returns incomplete data for certain questions, these questions will not be displayed in the table in order to only provide complete question data.

When you make a search, the results will be displayed in a table. In the table, the answer column is hidden so that you can try to answer the question. To reveal the answer, simply hover your mouse over the column and the answer will appear. You can also save a question you like to your favorites by clicking anywhere in that question's row. You can easily see your saved questions by clicking the "SHOW FAVORITES!" button. Happy searching! Alex Trebek approves!
